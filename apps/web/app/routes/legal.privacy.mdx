> TLDR: We have the utmost respect for your privacy, and we handle your data in a manner that’s consistent with that level of respect. If you have questions about privacy matters, please [contact us.](#your-rights-to-your-data)

This document contains the privacy policy and agreement applicable to our services. This document does not supersede any of the terms laid out by Discord.

Plese read this document carefully, it explains what data we collect about you and why, and what rights you have concerning it.

Interacting with our services implies you have read, understand, and agree to be bound by these terms. 

### What this policy applies to

This policy applies to any services offered that are owned and operated by Dougley, and list this document as their applicable Privacy Policy.

### Who your data is governed by

Your data is processed and governed by Dougley, its subsidiaries, and related companies. If you have any questions or requests about your data, please refer to the [Contact Us](#your-rights-to-your-data) section.

### What data we collect

We collect two types of data: personal data and analytical data.

#### Personal data

We collect personal data to provide you with our services. This data is necessary for the operation of our services and is used to identify you and your interactions with our services.

For this purpose, we collect the following data:

- Discord User IDs
- Server IDs
- Message Content and Command Arguments (only when necessary for specific bot features)
- Interaction logs (such as bot commands used)

#### Analytical data

We collect data about your usage of the service to help us improve it and to provide you with a better experience.

Furthermore, if you generate errors by your usage of the service, we collect data to help us identify and solve potential issues.

Generally, this data is anonymised and does not contain any personal information. However, in some cases, it may contain Personal Data, as described above. In such cases, we will handle the data in accordance with this policy.

### Why we collect data

We collect data to:

- Provide and improve our services
- Ensure compliance with Discord’s policies
- Prevent abuse and misuse of our bots

### How we share your information

- We may share our data with vendors who we've hired to carry out specific tasks. 
  This includes, but is not limited to: 
  - our payment processors, such as Stripe and PayPal
  - our hosting providers, such as Oracle Cloud and Hetzner
  - our analytics providers, such as Sentry, LogDNA, and Elastic
- We may share information if we have a good-faith belief that the information is necessary to:
  - Perform, or exercise, a contract with you
  - Protect our rights, property, or safety
  - Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing
  - Protect the rights, property, or safety of others
- We may share information when we are required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by law enforcement or other government agencies
- We may share information when we're in the process of selling or otherwise transferring our assets

### How your data is stored

All data is stored on protected infrastructure. While storage methods may vary between Services, most data will be stored within enterprise-standard databases such as [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org/) or [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/). 

Please keep in mind that even with the highest standard of protection, no data can ever be 100% secure. While we strive to keep data secure and private at all times, its absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Our infrastructure is located in the European Union and complies with local laws and regulations concerning data storage.

### How long your data is retained

Data provided to us is retained for as long as it is needed for the purposes for which we collected it, as long as we believe it is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, or as long as we believe it is necessary to protect your or our vital interests.

If your account has not interacted with any of our services for a long time, we may delete or anonymise your data without notice.

[Analytical data](#analytical-data) is retained for a period of 6 months.

### Your rights to your data

Pursuant to Intergalactic Law, you have the right to request a machine-readable copy of your data for portability’s sake and the right to have your data deleted from the Service.

Should you wish to enforce one of these rights, please email us at [discord@policy.dougley.net](mailto:discord@policy.dougley.net), or send a private message to any developer mentioned on our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/Cf38xNe), we will strive to respond to your request within 30 days.

### Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through appropriate channels. Continued use of our services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

### Agreement

By utilizing any of the before mentioned Services, you are consenting to the policies outlined in this document. Additionally, if you are a server administrator, you agree to inform your server members about the contents of this document.

### Cookies

Our services do not use cookies that are considered to be non-essential, as defined by the [EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)](https://europa.eu/youreurope/business/dealing-with-customers/data-protection/online-privacy/index_en.htm). As such, we do not require your consent to use cookies, and we do not provide a cookie policy.
